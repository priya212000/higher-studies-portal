Policy Analyst

  • Salary Info - Policy analysts make an average of $58,000 per year.
  • Job Availability - It is difficult to be self employed in this field. Most available jobs are in the federal government, and it is competitive to get into.
  • Education Requirement- One would need a bachelor’s degree, usually in a field related to statistics, public policy, public administration or politics. A master’s degree is also expected. Internships and work experience are also very important
